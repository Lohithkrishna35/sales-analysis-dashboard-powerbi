# Sales & Shipment Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales and shipment performance across products, geographies, and sales representatives. The dashboard provides business insights through KPI monitoring, trend analysis, shipment distribution tracking, and profitability reporting, enabling data-driven decision-making.

## Objectives

* Monitor overall sales, shipments, and profitability.
* Analyze product-wise and region-wise performance.
* Track shipment distribution patterns.
* Evaluate salesperson performance using key business metrics.
* Identify high-performing products and profit-driving segments.

## Key Features

### Executive KPI Dashboard

* Total Sales Amount
* Total Boxes Shipped
* Total Shipment Count
* Total Profit
* Profit Percentage

### Sales Trend Analysis

* Current Year vs Previous Year sales comparison.
* Shipment trend tracking over time.
* Monthly performance monitoring.

### Geographic Analysis

* Sales contribution by country/region.
* Market performance comparison across locations.

### Product Performance Analysis

* Top-performing products by revenue.
* Product profitability analysis.
* Product-wise sales contribution.

### Salesperson Performance Tracking

* Revenue generated by each salesperson.
* Shipment volume comparison.
* Individual profit percentage evaluation.

### Shipment Distribution Analysis

* Shipment quantity distribution across orders.
* Operational insights into shipment patterns.
